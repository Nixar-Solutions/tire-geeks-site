## 4. Problems & Pain Points

**ICP A core challenge** 🟢
Worn tires + tight cash + no credit access = forced to keep driving on unsafe rubber, or get denied at the chain shop's financing counter.

**ICP B core challenge** 🟢
Chain shops don't understand custom fitment ("the technician in the back may have been mounting tires for three weeks"). Going from shop to shop for lift / wheels / tires / alignment costs time and risks errors. Online wheel buys arrive with the wrong offset.

**Why current solutions fall short** 🟢
*From `best-tire-shop-sacramento` blog post:*
- "Chain stores rely almost entirely on database lookups… the moment you have a leveling kit, aftermarket wheels, a lift, spacers, or even a different trim level than what is in the database, those recommendations fall apart."
- "Surprise disposal fees, hidden shop charges, or magically appearing environmental surcharges that inflate your bill by 15 percent at checkout."
- "Wait two weeks for a tire appointment like you would at a dealership."

**Cost of inaction** 🟢
- Safety: hydroplaning risk on I-5 / W-X interchange; longer stopping distance; tire blowouts
- Money: misalignment burns through tires 25%+ faster; wrong-offset wheels stress bearings
- Time: bouncing between three shops to do one job
- Build velocity: ICP B can't get the build they want because of cash gap or shop fragmentation

**Emotional tension** 🟡
- ICP A: shame around credit, fear about kids in the rain, frustration at being denied or upsold
- ICP B: anxiety that the shop won't "get" the build, frustration when a chain installer scratches a $400 wheel, pride about the finished result

### Glossary of product-specific terms 🟢

| Term | Plain meaning |
|---|---|
| **Offset / backspacing** | How far the wheel mounting surface sits from centerline; determines flush/poke/tuck |
| **Hub-centric ring** | Fills gap between wheel center bore and vehicle hub; eliminates vibration |
| **Bolt pattern** | Lug count × diameter (e.g., 6×135, 5×114.3) |
| **Camber / caster / toe** | The three alignment angles |
| **Leveling kit** | 1.5–2.5" front-only lift to eliminate factory rake |
| **Lift kit** | 3–6"+ full vehicle raise for clearance / bigger tires |
| **Coilovers** | Adjustable spring-and-shock combined unit |
| **Air ride / bagged** | Air-spring suspension with on-the-fly height adjustment |
| **Rake** | Factory front-lower-than-rear stance (what leveling kits eliminate) |
| **TPMS** | Tire pressure monitoring sensors |
| **Stance / VIP** | Sub-genres of low/flush enthusiast builds |
| **Strut spacer / coil spacer / torsion bar key** | Three types of leveling kit by suspension design |
| **Rubber** | Slang for tires (used in service-page copy) |
| **CVC 24008** | California slam law — frame can't be lower than wheel rim |

## 11. Style Guide

**Capitalization** 🟢
- Headlines / display headers: **ALL CAPS**, always (Bebas Neue / Oswald display fonts)
- Section headers in body: Title Case
- Tagline / slogan / phone-number CTAs: ALL CAPS
- Sentence case in body paragraphs

**Brand-color word styling** 🟢
When referring to the brand mark in text: "**TIRE**" in red (#D42B2B) and "**GEEKS**" in blue (#1E88C7). In running prose, just write "Tire Geeks".

**Punctuation** 🟢
- Em-dash (—) is the signature break punctuation. Use unspaced or spaced consistently within a piece.
- Avoid semicolons.
- Oxford comma: use it.
- Numbers $1,000+ get commas. Phone numbers get parens: (916) 800-8786.
- Use 5,000 not "5K" in body copy. "5K" is OK in social/casual.

**Numbers** 🟢
- Spell out one through nine in body prose ("nine reasons"); use numerals for 10+ ("10,000 installations") and always for prices, sizes, percentages, distances.
- Tire/wheel sizes always numeric: 33-inch tires, 22x12 wheels, 6×135 bolt pattern.

**Phone numbers** 🟢
- Florin Road: **(916) 800-8786** — use this when the location is unspecified or when the Florin location is the subject
- Arden Way: **(916) 913-8786** — only when explicitly referring to Arden Way
- `tel:+19168008786` and `tel:+19169138786` for the corresponding raw formats

**Addresses** 🟢
- Florin Road: **3020 Florin Road, Sacramento, CA 95822**
- Arden Way: **2245 Arden Way, Sacramento, CA 95825**
- Hours: **Mon–Sat: 9AM–7PM** (Sun: Closed) — use en-dash between days, no space before/after AM/PM

**Preferred terminology** 🟢
| Use | Don't use |
|---|---|
| Walk in / walk-in | Schedule an appointment |
| Tires | Rubber (in formal copy; OK in casual) |
| Custom wheels | Aftermarket rims |
| Lift kit / leveling kit | Suspension upgrade |
| Lowering kit / coilovers / air ride | Lowered suspension |
| Mounting & balancing | Installation (alone — too vague) |
| Acima / 100 days no interest | "Buy now pay later" (too generic) |
